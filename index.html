How to pack rucksack

  1. Open the ruck sack and pack the sleeping bag at bottom
  2. Pack the heavy stuff like shoes in the middle
  3. Pack the light stuff at the top
  4. The accessories should be packed in a pouch and kept in the top pocket
  5. the water bottles shuld be in the side pocket
